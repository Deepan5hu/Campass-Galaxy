Rental Head Lamps

Please Login to booking systems to get your trek details !!!


Headlamps are essentials on a trek. When you are up in the night for a quick visit to the restroom, or you are trying to find the zip to close your sleeping bag in the tent, headlamps are a must on a High Altitude Trek.

The headlamp you get is 210 – 910 lumens. This should suffice on a high altitude trek.

The strap is adjustable and we have tested the durability of the strap on the trek. This headlamp should fit nicely around your head.
The buttons are easily available on the top.

Specifications

Material: ABS
LED Type: 3W COB LED
Battery: 3*AAA
Lumen: 210 lm – 910 lm
Size: 67*45*42mm
Weight: 47g

Features
  • Lightweight, Handy, Swivel mounted to adjust the angle of light
  • Adjustable elasticated headband
  • Multi-Mode lighting from bright/dim/flashing via the power switch
