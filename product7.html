On a High Altitude Trek, you lose body very quickly from your head. We have
observed that this causes a lot of issues as the trek progresses. It becomes
extremely important for you to keep the head covered at all times.

A fleece cap is the best gear you can use for that. The fleece material gives
you the extra warmth required and the cap fits snugly over your ears.

This fleece cap is 215 GSM. This level of fleece thickness will suffice in the
Himalayan cold.
